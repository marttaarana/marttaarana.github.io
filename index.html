Assignment 2 · April 2026 · Data Story

Proposition 47 and its Unintended Consequences

California's Prop 47 passed in 2014 with a clear mandate: stop treating addiction as a crime. Drug arrests in San Francisco fell sharply — exactly as intended. But the data tells a second story. In the same years and the same neighborhoods, larceny surged by 82%. Was this a coincidence, or a substitution? We followed the numbers to find out.

About this site

This is the course website for Group 26 of Social Data Analysis and Visualization at DTU (42578), Spring 2026. The course covers computational social science, data storytelling, and interactive visualization using Python and web technologies. Our assignments analyze the San Francisco Police Department's historical crime dataset (2003–2017, ~2 million incidents).
